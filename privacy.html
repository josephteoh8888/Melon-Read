Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

We don’t collect your data

Melon is a private social app. Your posts, messages, photos, and friends list are stored only on your device and the devices of people you explicitly share with — never on our servers.

No tracking. No ads. No analytics.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We cannot see what you post or who you talk to.

End-to-end encryption

All communication between devices is end-to-end encrypted. Only you and your friends can read your messages.
